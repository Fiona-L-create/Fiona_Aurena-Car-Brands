Welcome To Aurena Car Brands

We organize financing for our clients in partnership with reputable financial and banking institutions. The interest rates are competitive starting from 13% per year on a reducing balance, with flexible monthly installments up to 48 months. For a used saloon car, commercial pick-up and (or) cargo vehicle financing is up to 80% with a maximum repayment period of 48 Months thats 4 years; for a brand new one the financing is up to 80% with a maximum repayment period of 5 years.

The requirements for qualifications :
For an individual:
  • Complete the loan application form
  • 6 months (latest) original bank statements
  • Copy of PIN and national ID
  • 3 months current payslips (if employed)
  • Original KRA search certificate for used vehicles
  • Original market valuation report
For a business / company:
  • 6 months current bank statements
  • Audited accounts for the last 3 years
  • Copy of PIN and certificate of incorporation / registration
  • Board resolution
  • Memo & articles of association
